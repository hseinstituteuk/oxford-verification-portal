About Certificate Verification

The University of Oxford maintains a secure database of all awarded certificates and degrees. This verification system allows employers, academic institutions, and other authorized parties to confirm the authenticity of Oxford University qualifications.

Verification Process

  1. Enter the unique certificate number found on the document
  2. Our system will search official university records
  3. Verified results will display the certificate holder's details
  4. For additional verification, contact the Examinations and Assessments office

Security Features

  • Holographic university seal
  • Microprinted text
  • Unique certificate number
  • UV-reactive elements
  • Security watermark paper
